Class implementing a family of products (i.e., a product line). #lt;br#gt; A family is represented by its products (or configurations). #lt;br#gt; In featured modal contract automata, partial products are also considered, also known as sub-families. #lt;br#gt; In a partial product not all features are rendered as required or forbidden. #lt;br#gt; The sub-products are partially ordered.#lt;br#gt;  The formal definitions can be found in:<br/><ul>
<li>Basile, D. et al., 2020. Controller synthesis of service contracts with variability. Science of Computer Programming, vol. 187, pp. 102344. (<a href="file://https://doi.org/10.1016/j.scico.2019.102344">https://doi.org/10.1016/j.scico.2019.102344</a>)</li></ul> <br/><br/>
